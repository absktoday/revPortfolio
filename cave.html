The Problem

The domain of artistic event management is decentralized and usually organized on a 1-on-1 basis (with one side being the artist, the other being the venue).This lack of a centralized platform to connect local artists, venues, and potential consumers creates an inefficient economy that limits the growth of local art industries as a whole. Local artists have to network on their own in order to find venues interested in performers, often relying on cold calls or visits. Most of the time, this impedes the artists from dedicating full time to their true calling. Similarly, venues looking for artists that are appropriate for their clientele had no centralized listings of available artists to select from, and no real way to evaluate any prospective performers. There are some existing applications that feature events, but these primarily highlight large events with well known artists; however, any information about smaller, less formal events is either nonexistent, or buried and difficult to browse. We believe our app delivers where other apps have failed to revolutionize and grow the local art scene in the same way technology has transformed other industries such as dating and the ride-sharing economy.

The goal of this project was to release a fully functional app for both iOS and Android where artists and venues can connect to create and promote events/shows, while the general public can find these happening in their area. Our app provides artists with exposure to both prospective spaces to express themselves, and a fan base to be present for them; thus allowing artists to make a living sharing their creative work with the world. Venues looking for local artists to enrich the experience their establishment provides can browse through listings of performers looking for gigs to find the type of artists their clientele might enjoy, as well as seeing reviews of their past performances to ensure that the experience provided will be a positive one for their patrons. Finally, our app brought a whole new level of entertainment offering for the general public.
